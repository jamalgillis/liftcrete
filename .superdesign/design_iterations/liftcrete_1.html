Our Process

How It Works

1

Drill

Strategically drill 5/8" holes (the size of a dime) through the sunken slab.

2

Pump & Lift

Inject self-expanding polyurethane resin under the slab to fill voids and raise it to its original position.

3

Patch

Patch the small drill holes with fast-setting concrete for a clean, nearly invisible finish.

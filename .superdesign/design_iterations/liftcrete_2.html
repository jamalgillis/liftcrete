OUR 3-STEP REPAIR PROCESS

Precision engineering meets efficient execution.

01

DRILL

Small 5/8" holes (about the size of a dime) are strategically drilled through the slab to create injection points.

02

PUMP & LIFT

A self-expanding polyurethane resin is pumped under pressure, filling voids and raising the slab to its original level.

03

PATCH

The holes are patched with fast-setting concrete, leaving a clean, nearly invisible finish compared to traditional mudjacking.
